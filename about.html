Our Story

Meadowbrook Pharmacy was founded in 2009 by Abdul and Myra Rasul, with a mission to bring back personalized care in an increasingly impersonal industry. Abdul, a seasoned pharmacist, and Myra saw a need for a pharmacy that prioritizes patients over profit, creating a trusted healthcare partner for the community.
